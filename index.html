The University of Jammu

'A+' Grade University by National Assessment & Accreditation Councilof India

University came into existence in 1969 vide Kashmir and Jammu Universities Act 1969 following bifurcation of theerstwhile University of Jammu and Kashmir. The University provides instructions in such branches of learning as it deems fit and makes provision for research and the advancement and dissemination of knowledge. he University stands for spiritual and material elements in life,thirst for knowledge and virtue under the backdrop of holy peaks of Trikuta Hills.
